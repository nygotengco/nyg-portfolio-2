I am a 22-year-old passion-driven fresher with a background in front-end development and graphical user interface design. Recalling all my prior experiences at the university, I am typically in charge on the front-end development of our projects and activities, and that made me more interested to explore different graphical user interface designs. This experience seemed really far from what I've wanted, but this made me grow professionally and realized what a real IT industry is like compared to when you are still in the university.
